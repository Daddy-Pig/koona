Terms and Conditions:

A child is taken in to the center after health inspection of body temp and signs and symptoms of fever, colds, and/or cough. The accompanying guardian / parent must comply the following at the reception desk:

  • Present any government-issued ID
  • Fill up admission form & supplies checklist
  • Claim Slip / Official Receipt

Requirements upon Admission:

  1. Child's supplies such as:
    • Feeding bottle / utensils
    • Milk formula / Soft food formula / Prescribed snacks if any
    • Diapers and wipes
    • Change of clothing
    • Medication if any and medical condition worth noting by the care personnel
